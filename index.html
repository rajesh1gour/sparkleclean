About Extreme Cleaners

The First Professional Cleaning Startup in Barak Valley

Extreme Cleaners was founded in May 2020 during one of the most challenging periods in modern history—the COVID-19 pandemic. What began as a vision to provide professional, reliable, and hygienic cleaning services has grown into a trusted cleaning company serving homes, businesses, and commercial establishments across the Barak Valley.

As the first professional cleaning startup in the Barak Valley region, we take pride in setting new standards for cleanliness, hygiene, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to deliver high-quality cleaning solutions using modern equipment, trained professionals, and industry-approved cleaning practices.

We specialize in residential cleaning, commercial cleaning, deep cleaning, post-construction cleaning, office cleaning, floor care, and sanitization services. Every project we undertake is handled with attention to detail, professionalism, and a commitment to excellence.

At Extreme Cleaners, we believe that a clean environment is essential for health, productivity, and peace of mind. Whether it is a home, office, retail store, hospital, hotel, or industrial facility, our team works tirelessly to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness.

Today, we continue to expand our services while staying true to the values that inspired our journey—quality, reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
